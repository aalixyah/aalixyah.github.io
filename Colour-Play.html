Colour Play

❮ ❯

Entitled Colour Play as part of my university assignment I created a photographic book based on the art of data. The project focuses on what constitutes as an image, drawing inspiration from the DADA movement and various models of colour theory. The images in the book are created through the manipulation of code analysing the relationship between the analog and digital. Within the spectrum of images the book explores the visualisation of music and text as a photograph through lyric matrixes, graphs and design.
